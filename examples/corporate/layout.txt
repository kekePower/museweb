The layout is strictly defined by `/style.css`.
- The main wrapper is a `<div class="container">`.
- The header is a `<div id="header">`.
- The main content area is a `<div id="main-content">`.
- Use a single `<h1>` for the page title. Use `<p>` for text.
- The structure is rigid and boxy, reflecting a standard corporate template.
